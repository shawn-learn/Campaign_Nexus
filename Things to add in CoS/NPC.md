In the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition adventure *Curse of Strahd*, **Stanimir** is a Vistani NPC who serves as a primary hook to draw player characters into the demiplane of Barovia.

---

## Identity and Role

* **Faction:** He is a Vistani elder, a mage (Chaotic Neutral), and a loyal follower of Madame Eva.
* **Family:** He travels with his daughter, Damia, and his son, Ratka.
* **Function:** In the "Mysterious Visitors" adventure hook, Stanimir and his troupe camp outside the town of Daggerford in the Forgotten Realms. He uses his storytelling and magic to entice the party into traveling back to Barovia with his caravan.

---

## The Tale of the Prince

During the introductory encounter, Stanimir performs a magical demonstration by spitting wine into a campfire to turn the flames green, projecting illusory silhouettes to illustrate his story.

He shares the legendary Vistani lore explaining their history with Strahd von Zarovich:

* **The Rescue:** Centuries ago, a wounded soldier (who was secretly a prince) staggered into their camp. The Vistani nursed him back to health and escorted him safely back to his homeland, protecting him from enemy assassins.
* **The Pact:** In gratitude, the prince promised that the Vistani would always be safe in his domain, allowed to enter and leave the valley's deadly mists at will.
* **The Mission:** Stanimir explains that a dark curse has since fallen upon this "noble prince," turning him into a tyrant. He urges the adventurers to travel to the Tser Pool Camp to meet the seer Madame Eva, who can read their fortunes and help break the curse.

## Political & Faction Leaders

### Urwin & Danika Martikov

* **Location:** N2. Blue Water Inn (Vallaki)
* **Role:** Proprietors of the Blue Water Inn and secret leaders of the **Keepers of the Feather**, a faction of wereravens resisting Strahd. They act as vital allies, info brokers, and potential safe-haven providers for the party.

### Baron Vargas Vallakovich

* **Location:** N3. Burgomaster's Mansion (Vallaki)
* **Role:** The delusional Burgomaster of Vallaki. He enforces mandatory, weekly festivals (e.g., the Festival of the Blazing Sun) under the belief that forced happiness keeps Strahd’s dread at bay. He is a primary political actor in Vallaki's power struggle.

### Lady Fiona Wachter

* **Location:** N4. Wachterhaus (Vallaki)
* **Role:** A noblewoman, loyal Strahd sympathizer, and leader of a local devil-worshiping cult. She actively plots to overthrow Baron Vargas Vallakovich to seize control of Vallaki, offering a direct counter-faction to the Burgomaster.

## Key Allies & Tragic Figures

### Ismark "The Lesser" Kolyanovich

* **Location:** E2. Bildrath's Mercantile / Burgomaster's Mansion (Village of Barovia)
* **Role:** Ireena Kolyana's brother and the newly appointed, overwhelmed Burgomaster of the Village of Barovia following their father’s death. He recruits the party to escort Ireena to safety.

### Victor Vallakovich

* **Location:** N3t. Attic Workroom (Vallaki)
* **Role:** The Baron’s socially isolated, sinister son. He is a self-taught mage attempting to construct a functional teleportation circle to escape Barovia, testing it with highly unstable and lethal results on the household staff.

### Stella Wachter

* **Location:** N4q. Stella's Bedroom (Vallaki)
* **Role:** Lady Fiona Wachter’s daughter. Following a disastrous courtship with Victor Vallakovich, she suffered a psychological break and now believes she is a cat.

### Sir Godfrey Gwilym

* **Location:** Q37. Knights of the Order (Argynvostholt)
* **Role:** A fallen knight of the Order of the Silver Dragon. Unlike the hostile Vladimir Horngaard, Godfrey retains his sanity and goodness despite being a revenant. He is a highly capable combat ally if recruited.

### The Mad Mage of Mount Baratok

* **Location:** M. Mount Baratok
* **Role:** A powerful wizard (canonically Mordenkainen) who lost his mind and his spellbook after a failed rebellion against Strahd. He wanders the shores of Lake Zarovich, hostile and deeply paranoid, unless cured of his madness.

### Emil Toranescu

* **Location:** K75a. Dungeon Cell (Castle Ravenloft) / Werewolf Den
* **Role:** A werewolf pack leader imprisoned by Strahd in the dungeons of Castle Ravenloft after a leadership dispute engineered by his rival, Kiril Stoyanovich.

## Notable Villains & Monsters

### Kiril Stoyanovich

* **Location:** Z7. Werewolf Den
* **Role:** The brutal, power-hungry leader of the children-kidnapping werewolf pack. He is loyal to Strahd and serves as the primary antagonist for the werewolf hunting subplot.

### Morgantha

* **Location:** O4. Old Bonegrinder
* **Role:** A night hag who poses as a kindly old woman selling addictive "Dream Pastries" in Vallaki and the Village of Barovia, using child abductions to fuel her coven's pastry production.

## The Wizard of Wines & Yester Hill

### Davian Martikov

* **Location:** W. Wizard of Wines Winery
* **Role:** The stubborn patriarch of the Martikov family and owner of the winery. He has a tense relationship with his son Urwin (of Vallaki's Blue Water Inn). He tasks the players with reclaiming the winery from druids and locating three stolen magic seeds that keep the vineyard fertile.

---

## Vallaki's Underworld & Key Players

### Henrik van der Voort

* **Location:** N6. Coffin Maker's Shop (Vallaki)
* **Role:** A cowardly local coffin maker whom Strahd’s agents (via Rahadin) blackmailed into hiding vampire spawns in his attic. He is central to the "St. Andral's Feast" event, where players must retrieve the stolen bones of St. Andral to keep the local church consecrated.

### Father Lucian Petrovich

* **Location:** N1. St. Andral's Church (Vallaki)
* **Role:** The local priest of Vallaki. He is a reliable, good-aligned ally who desperately seeks the party's help in recovering the stolen protective relics of the church to prevent an impending vampire attack.

### Ernst Larnak

* **Location:** N4. Wachterhaus (Vallaki)
* **Role:** Lady Fiona Wachter’s loyal spy and servant. He tracks the characters' movements through Vallaki and reports their actions back to her, serving as a constant tail unless dealt with.

## Tragic Souls & Trapped Spirits

### Rose & Thorn (Rosavalda & Thornbold Durst)

* **Location:** E. Death House (Village of Barovia)
* **Role:** The ghost children of the Durst family. Found in the streets of the Village of Barovia, they plead with the adventurers to go inside their home to banish a "monster" in the basement, acting as the lures for the optional introductory dungeon.

### Lief Lipsiege

* **Location:** K30. Accountant's Office (Castle Ravenloft)
* **Role:** Strahd’s long-suffering, non-vampire accountant. Chained to his desk, he is a commoner who is deeply bitter about his endless job of logging Strahd’s immense wealth and taxes, but remains too terrified of his master to assist players without high persuasion.

### Sir Klutz Tripalots

* **Location:** K84. Crypt 33 (Castle Ravenloft)
* **Role:** A ghostly knight of the Order of the Silver Dragon who died in a comically clumsy manner (tripping on his own shield during battle). He can be summoned as a phantom warrior ally to assist the party within Castle Ravenloft.

## Vistani & Dusk Elf Factions

### Luvash & Arrigal

* **Location:** N9. Vistani Camp (Vallaki)
* **Role:** Two Vistani brothers who lead the camp outside Vallaki.
* **Luvash** is loud, aggressive, and currently distraught over his missing daughter, Arabelle.
* **Arrigal** is a highly dangerous assassin who secretly reports directly to Strahd, serving as a double agent.



### Arabelle

* **Location:** L. Lake Zarovich
* **Role:** Luvash's young daughter and a descendant of Madame Eva. Players find her trapped in a sack, about to be drowned in Lake Zarovich by a superstitious local fisherman named Bluto. If rescued, she can serve as a potential fated ally.
