Beyond the main campaign villains (Strahd, Baba Lysaga, and the Abbot), several unique, named monsters and high-threat entities serve as regional bosses or critical mechanical threats throughout Barovia.

---

## Named & Unique Monsters

### Wintersplinter

* **Type:** Tree Blight (Huge Plant)
* **Location:** Yester Hill
* **Role:** A colossal, destructive plant monster summoned by the druids of Yester Hill using one of the Wizard of Wines’ stolen magic gems. If the ritual is not stopped, Wintersplinter is sent to physically destroy the winery.

### Doru

* **Type:** Vampire Spawn
* **Location:** E5. Church Underfloor (Village of Barovia)
* **Role:** The son of Father Donavich. After participating in the Mad Mage's failed rebellion, Doru was turned into a vampire spawn by Strahd and sent back to torture his father. He is locked in the church cellar, starving and screaming for blood.

### Neferon

* **Type:** Arcanaloth (Fiend)
* **Location:** X5a. Guard Tower (The Amber Temple)
* **Role:** A cunning, jackal-headed fiend hiding inside the statue of god of secrets in the Amber Temple. He uses *greater invisibility*, *fireball*, and *chain lightning* to snipe intruders across the temple's main hall.

### Sangzor

* **Type:** Giant Goat
* **Location:** Tsolenka Pass
* **Role:** A legendary, bloodthirsty giant goat with a stone-hard skull that terrorizes travelers along the snowy cliffs of Tsolenka Pass.

### The Roc of Mount Baratok

* **Type:** Roc (Gargantuan Monstrosity)
* **Location:** Mount Baratok / Tsolenka Pass
* **Role:** A massive bird of prey with a 200-foot wingspan that hunts across Lake Zarovich and the high peaks of Barovia, capable of picking up horses, wolves, or player characters.

## Faction & Environment Threats

### The Brides of Strahd (Ludmilla, Anastasya, Volenta)

* **Type:** Vampire Spawns (with unique stats/abilities)
* **Location:** Castle Ravenloft / Dynamic Encounters
* **Role:** Strahd's consort-commanders. While mechanically classified as vampire spawns in the raw book, they function as distinct tactical sub-bosses, each representing different aspects of Strahd's court (e.g., Ludmilla as the coordinator, Anastasya as the social manipulator, Volenta as the sadistic executioner).

### The Shadows of Argynvostholt

* **Type:** Phantom Warriors / Revenants
* **Location:** Q. Argynvostholt
* **Role:** The undead remnants of the Order of the Silver Dragon. Led by the vengeful Vladimir Horngaard, these spectral knights are locked in a state of eternal hatred, attacking anyone who seeks to lay Argynvost's spirit to rest unless the dragon's beacon is lit.

### The Vestiges (The Dark Powers)

* **Type:** Formless Entities / Sarcophagi
* **Location:** X. The Amber Temple
* **Role:** The ancient, dead gods or malevolent slivers trapped inside amber sarcophagi. While they cannot be fought in standard combat, they act as mechanical narrative hazards, offering dark gifts (corrupting boons) to players in exchange for their humanity.
