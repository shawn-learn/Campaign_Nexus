In the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition adventure *Curse of Strahd*, magic items are split between three dynamically-placed primary quest items and a large number of items with fixed locations throughout Barovia.

---

## Fortunes of Ravenloft (Variable Locations)

The primary quest items do not have fixed locations. Their placement is determined dynamically at the start of the campaign via the Tarokka Card reading performed by Madam Eva.

### Tome of Strahd

* **Description:** A narrative journal detailing Strahd's transformation into a vampire and his obsession with Tatyana.

### Holy Symbol of Ravenkind

* **Description:** A legendary platinum amulet shaped like a sun, featuring a central crystal. Requires attunement by a good-aligned Cleric or Paladin. It provides abilities to hold vampires, aid turn undead features, and generate true sunlight.

### The Sunsword

* **Description:** A sentient, chaotic good blade of pure radiance (+2 longsword with finesse) that inflicts radiant damage and emits true sunlight.

### Town of Vallaki (Chapter 5)

* **Hat of Disguise:** Carried by Rictavio at the Blue Water Inn.
* **Ring of Mind Shielding:** Carried by Rictavio at the Blue Water Inn.
* **Bag of Tricks (Gray):** Guest Room (Area N2h), Blue Water Inn.
* **Elixir of Health (x2) & Potions of Healing (x3):** Hidden inside Area N2h, Blue Water Inn.
* **Magic Mirror of Summon Assassin:** Burgomaster's Mansion, Closet (Area N3p).
* **Victor's Spellbook:** Burgomaster's Mansion, Attic (Area N3t).
* **Ring of Warmth:** Carried by Kasimir Velikov at the Vistani Camp (Area N9a).
* **Kasimir's Spellbook:** A leather-bound book kept inside Kasimir's hovel (Area N9a), in the dusk elf encampment southwest of Vallaki. Contains his prepared mage spells (Appendix D / MM base template) plus the extras named in his hovel text:
  * *Cantrips:* fire bolt, light, mage hand, prestidigitation (errata removed ray of frost)
  * *1st:* arcane lock, comprehend languages, detect magic, identify, mage armor, magic missile, shield
  * *2nd:* darkness (added by the hovel text), invisibility, locate object, misty step, suggestion
  * *3rd:* counterspell, dispel magic, fireball, fly, nondetection
  * *4th:* greater invisibility, polymorph
  * *5th:* cone of cold, legend lore
* **Bones of St. Andral (Plot Item):** Coffinmaker's Shop, Attic (Area N6e).

## Additional Magic Items Found in the Master Quest Notes

These didn't appear in the original pass above, but turned up while checking
the `cos/` DM notes for anything missing.

* **Manual of Flesh Golems:** Found alongside the Stone Carving of a Walrus
  during a search of S17 (Abbot's Quarters), Abbey of Saint Markovia.
* **Khazan's Phylactery:** Hidden in a secret compartment behind a suit of
  armour at Van Richten's Tower. Destructible only by a strike from the Staff
  of Power.
* **Magic Gem (x3):** Custom items (reflavoured *Cone of Regeneration*, 1
  charge/dawn) stolen from the Wizard of Wines. One is embedded in the
  Druids' Strahd statue at Yester Hill (Y3); one is kept in Baba Lysaga's
  hut at the Ruins of Berez (U3); the third's location is deliberately left
  a mystery.

## Mundane (Non-Magical) Objects

Named, non-magical props tied to specific plot points or locations — not
magic items, but worth tracking like the ones above.

* **Deed to the Mill:** Found in Death House. Lets the party legally claim
  Old Bonegrinder from the night hags (DC 20 Persuasion/Intimidation).
* **Groom Figurine:** A decoration in the Dining Hall of the Count (K36,
  Castle Ravenloft). Removing it from the room summons an invisible stalker.
* **Stone Carving of a Walrus:** Found during a search of S17 (Abbot's
  Quarters), Abbey of Saint Markovia — the Abbot's one memento of his mortal
  life before he was made a deva.
* **Ezmerelda's Prosthetic Leg:** Torn off and discarded in the Castle
  Ravenloft Torture Chamber (K76), underwater, if Strahd captures and
  tortures Ezmerelda.
* **Flamboyant Vistani Wagon:** Ezmerelda's covered wagon, at Van Richten's
  Tower. Knowing its command words lets the party drive it at a Fast pace
  (4 mph) with no penalty; sleeps the whole party but can't enter the deep
  Barovian woods.
